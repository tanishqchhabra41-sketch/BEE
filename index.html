Transformer Concepts

On Load, No Load & Auto Transformer

Introduction

Transformer transfers electrical energy using electromagnetic induction.

No Load Condition

Only no-load current flows in primary.

No Load Current

I₀ = Iₘ + Iw

No Load Losses

On Load Condition

Current flows in both windings

Current Relation

Losses on Load
